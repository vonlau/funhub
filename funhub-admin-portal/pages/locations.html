Locations

Manage cities and physical check-in spots used for location-based features in the app.

Cities
The list of cities available for user profiles and location-based filters. Used in Advanced Search to find users by region.
📍
Check-in Locations
Physical spots users can check in to (e.g. a store or event venue). Each location has a name, coordinates, and radius. Linked to QR Scan or Custom Action mission tasks.
