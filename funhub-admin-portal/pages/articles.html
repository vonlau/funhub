The content management section — create, organise, and publish news, guides, and events visible to users in the app.

Content
📄
Articles (Index)
Create, edit, publish, or archive articles. Each article has a title, body, categories, tags, status, and publish date. Only Published articles appear in the app.
StatusWhat it means
PublishedLive and visible to users in the app
DraftBeing written — not visible to users yet
ArchivedRemoved from the app but kept in records
Organisation
📁
Categories
Group articles by topic (e.g. "News", "Events"). Users can browse or filter by category in the app.
🏷️
Tags
Extra labels for articles. Tags give users another way to search and find content beyond the main categories.
Automation
🔑
Expired Keywords & Auto Archive Keywords
Two tools to reduce manual clean-up.

Expired Keywords are old terms the system should ignore in search (e.g. outdated event names).
Auto Archive Keywords (e.g. "ended", "closed") automatically archive articles that contain those words — so you don't have to manually archive every finished promotion.

💡
Tip: Set Auto Archive Keywords like "ended" or "sold out" at the start of a campaign. The system will clean up old articles for you automatically.
