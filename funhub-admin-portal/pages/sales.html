FunCards (Products)
🎴
FunCard
A FunCard is something you sell in the app — a voucher, event pass, or partner product. Each has a name, price, images, and status.
SettingWhat it means
StatusPublished = visible & buyable · Draft = hidden · Archived = no longer sold
Collaboration Coin ConversionHow many coins the user earns when buying this product (default 1:1, or set Fixed / Percentage)
Purchase NotificationOptional message sent to user after payment. Choose a Notification Template. Placeholders: {product_name} {user_name} {amount} {transaction_no}
Collaboration Coin Conversion options
OptionHow it worksExample
None (default)RM 1 = 1 coinPay RM 100 → 100 coins
FixedAlways the same coin amount regardless of priceSet 30 → always 30 coins
PercentageCoins = % of amount paidSet 0.30 → pay RM 100 → 30 coins

Coin Packages
📦
Coin Packages
Purchasable Fun Coin bundles (e.g. "Pay RM 10, get 10 base + 2 bonus coins"). Managed here under Sales.
SettingWhat it means
PriceWhat the user pays (e.g. RM 19.90)
Base CoinsStandard coins always received
Bonus CoinsExtra coins for repeat purchases
First-Purchase BonusExtra coins only on the user's very first coin buy — package bonus does NOT apply on that first purchase to avoid double bonus
Purchase NotificationMessage sent after payment. Must choose a template. Placeholders: {package_name} {total_coins} {base_coins} {bonus_coins} {transaction_no}
⚙️ First vs Repeat Purchase Logic
  • First purchase — first-purchase bonus applies. Package bonus (+X coins) does NOT apply.
  • Repeat purchase — package bonus applies. First-purchase bonus does not.
  • Example: Starter Pack = 19 base + 5 bonus. First-purchase bonus = +20%. First time: 19 + 20% ≈ 23 coins. Next time: 19 + 5 = 24 coins.
