Transactions

A complete log of every financial movement — product purchases, coin buys, point changes, redemptions, and claims.

Transaction Types
🛍️
Product Purchases
Every FunCard purchase made in the app. Includes user, product, amount, date, and payment status.
🪙
Coin Transactions
All coin credits and debits — from purchases, admin adjustments, and collaboration conversions.
⭐
Point Transactions
All point credits and debits — from missions, promotions, and admin adjustments.
🎁
Redemptions
Users spending points or coins on rewards. Each record shows what was redeemed, by whom, and when.
📋
Claims
Users claiming rewards they've earned (e.g. from a collectible set or mission completion).
