Home Page — Banner Sections
🖼️
Banners — Home Page
The home screen has three distinct banner sections, each serving a different purpose. All are managed here.
1. Jumbo Section

The large hero banner at the top of the home screen. Used for big events, paid ad placements, and partner collaborations.

SettingDetail
PurposeFunHub big events · paid ad placement · partner collaborations
Max banners5 (only up to 5 can be active at once)
Each banner hasImage, title, link (campaign, article, or URL), active period, display order
ℹ️
Links accept both URLs and mobile routes. Every banner and placement across all sections supports either a full URL (e.g. https://...) or an internal mobile route (e.g. fundeal, fungo, nearby_food) to deep-link directly into a section of the app.
⚠️
Maximum 5 active banners. If you try to activate a 6th banner, it will not show. Archive or deactivate an existing one first.
2. Quick Access Section

Icon shortcuts that give users fast access to in-app pages — e.g. FunDeal, FunGo, Nearby – Food, Nearby – Leisure.

SettingDetail
PurposeIn-app page shortcuts — link directly to internal features or pages
Min placements5 (at least 5 must be active at all times)
ExamplesFunDeal · FunGo · Nearby – Food · Nearby – Leisure
Each item hasIcon, label, destination link, display order
⚠️
Minimum 5 placements required. The Quick Access section expects at least 5 items. Fewer than 5 will cause the layout to appear broken on the home screen.
3. Grid Section

A grid of smaller tiles below the Quick Access row. Used for ads, in-app promotions, and announcements.

SettingDetail
PurposeAds placement · in-app promos · announcements
Min placements3 (at least 3 must be active)
Each tile hasImage, title, link, active period, display order
⚠️
Minimum 3 tiles required. The grid layout requires at least 3 active tiles to render correctly.
