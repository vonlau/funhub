Approvals

A single queue of requests that need an admin to approve or reject before the action takes effect.

📋
What Approvals Is For
Currently used for point distribution and coin distribution requests. When a bulk points/coins action is submitted (e.g. via Advanced Search), it enters this queue instead of applying immediately — giving a second admin a chance to review before anything is credited.
⚙️ How it works
  • Each row shows what is being requested (e.g. "Give user X 500 points") and who submitted it.
  • You review the request and either Approve (the system credits the points/coins) or Reject (nothing happens).
  • Approvals keep a record of who approved what and when — useful for audit purposes.
  • Only admins who have been assigned the Approvals permission can see and action items in this queue.
⚠️
Assigned admins only. Not all admin accounts can approve. Make sure the right staff have the Approvals permission under Roles & Permissions.
